About us

It all began during the first nine days of August 1907 on Brownsea Island, Dorset. When Lord Baden-Powell held an experimental camp to try out his ideas and brought together 20 boys from a variety of backgrounds.

We are proud to be part of the Town that Scouting was born in and today we offer fun, challenge and adventure to 14 - 18 year-olds.

We enable young people from every background to reach their full potential through the unique experience of Scouting. They get the chance to enjoy new activities, make friends, develop skills, and make a positive impact in the community.
